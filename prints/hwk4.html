5.1 a) KKT Condition Verification

  1. All constraints satisfy $f_i(x*) <= 0$
  2. All lambdas are positive (satisfied above)
  3. $ \lambda_i * f_i(x*) = 0 $
  4. Gradient of Langrangian: $ \begin{bmatrix} 2x_1 - x_2 - 1 \\ 4x_2 - x_1 \end{bmatrix} + \lambda_1 \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} + \lambda_2 \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -4 \end{bmatrix}+ \lambda_3 \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 76 \end{bmatrix} $

a) Minimize cost $$p^T (u + c)$$ subject to $$ q_t \leq Q \\ c_t \leq C \\ c_t \geq -D \\ q_{t+1} = q_t + c_t q_1 = q_T + c_T \\ u_t + c_t \geq 0 $$
